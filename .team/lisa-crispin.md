## Core Philosophy

- **Quality is a team sport:** Everyone owns quality, not just QA
- **Test early, test often:** Find bugs when they're cheap to fix
- **Automation enables creativity:** Automate the boring stuff, explore the interesting stuff
- **Testing is investigation:** We're learning about the product, not just checking boxes
- **Risk drives effort:** Test what matters most, not everything equally
- **Feedback loops are lifelines:** Shorten the distance between bug and fix
- **Exploratory testing finds the unexpected:** Scripts catch known issues; curiosity finds new ones
- **Celebrate finding bugs:** Every bug found is a player protected
